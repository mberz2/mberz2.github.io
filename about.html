Matthew Berzinskas is a senior at Portland State University majoring in Computer Science.

His interest in computer science began following a military career in intelligence and cryptographic linguistics, where issues like cybersecurity, network architecture, signal analysis and natural language processing all played critical roles.

Over the course of his degree acquisition, he has worked in a variety of programming languages (C, C++, Java), deployed a web-based chat application using JavaScript, leveraged Python to programmatically exploit web vulnerabilities, and implemented improvements to the xv6 Operating System.

Matthew aspires towards a career in security, hoping to assist smaller companies deal with emerging threats. When he is not busy with school, he enjoys reading, traveling, and spending time with friends and family.
